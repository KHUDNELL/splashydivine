Why Doodles?

AFFECTIONATE

Doodles are sociable creatures. Great with children. Quiet times and snuggle times can be the best of times! A Doodle may be the ideal friend for a human. When properly socialized, they are an ideal family member.

HYPO-ALLERGENIC

Doodle dogs are bred as a great pet choice for allergy sufferers. They are less likely to have dander and are bred to shed less hair.

INTELLIGENT

They love mental and physical stimulation. Doodles can learn commands quickly and are eager to please. Introducing a leash and crate to your pet early, helps them easily adapt to training.

LOYAL

Doodles are protective and very smart. The activity of leash walking allows a Doodle greater social stimulation with their owners.

PLAYFUL

They can bring energy and family-fun into your home. Playtime is fun time! Family oriented Doodles are adaptable to an active human lifestyle.They are very intuitive and friendly creatures.

SUPERIOR

The combination of two different breeds often results in a healthier, more vigorous dog. Doodles are a combination of another breed with the ever-popular Poodle, a genetically diverse breed. And, genetic diversity tends to create healthier dogs. The hybrid F1 puppy is often superior to it's parents!

AussieDoodle

These are beautiful dogs that are a cross between the Australian Shepherd and the Poodle. They are energetic, very intelligent and easy to train. They are also extremely loyal to their children and family. They may have merle coloring, which is depicted by darker splotches of color on a lighter color background coat. The AussieDoodle is considered the "Einstein" of Doodles, because of the high level of intelligence of both breeds.

Cockapoo

Possibly the oldest of the designer dog breeds, the Cockapoo is a joyful mix of Miniature Poodle and Cocker Spaniel. They are people oriented, easy to train and often quiet. Cockapoos have a sweet temperament, are friendly, and very affectionate!

Poodle

Poodles are peaceful, sensitive dogs that are highly intelligent. They were originally bred as water retrievers and truffle hunters. The characteristic curly coat, which is hypoallergenic, serves a Poodle well to keep warm when swimming in chilly waters. They are happy, protective, and loyal fur friends.
